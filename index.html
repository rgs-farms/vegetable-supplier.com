About Kavali Family Farms

Four generations of passion for fresh, organic produce

Rooted in Tradition, Growing for the Future

The Kavali family has been cultivating vegetables for over 50 years, beginning with our great-grandparents in the fertile lands of Ranga Reddy district. What began as a small family plot has blossomed into RGS Vegetable Supplier, serving households and businesses across Hyderabad with the freshest seasonal produce.

We maintain traditional farming wisdom while adopting modern sustainable practices that ensure every vegetable we deliver is packed with natural goodness. Our produce is harvested at peak ripeness from our primary farm in Dharma Sagar and delivered quickly to maintain maximum freshness.

Today, we proudly supply to major retailers, restaurants, and online platforms while maintaining our family's commitment to quality and customer satisfaction that has sustained us for generations.
